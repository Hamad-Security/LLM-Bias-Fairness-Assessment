Detailed Comprehensive Analysis of Results:

Precision measures the fraction of recommended movies that users positively rated (>=4). Low precision indicates the need for better grounding. Fuzzy matching was used to account for slight title mismatches.

Log Popularity Difference shows whether recommendations favored popular or niche content. Negative values consistently indicated successful popularity bias reduction.

Hit Rate@5 and Hit Rate@10 measured whether users' liked movies were present within top-5 and top-10 recommended lists, respectively.

Overall, sensitive attribute prompts helped slightly in improving diversity and reducing popularity bias, while niche-focused prompt strategies were most effective.
